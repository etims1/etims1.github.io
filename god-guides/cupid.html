A Guide to Cupid

Cupid excells like most Huntes in lane-clear, he also has High Sustain due to to his Share the Love (2) ability, which heals and regenerates Mana and Flutter (3) his dash, allowing escape. Physical Damage is important to Cupid.

Abilities

Lovestruck (Passive)

Each basic attack hit gives Cupid a stack that increases his damage and the healing of his abilitie, 2% per stack, stacking a maximum of 8 times. This gives cupid a 16% maximum damage and healing increase. At this maximum Heart Bomb (1) and Fields of Love (4) apply a 1 second stun.

Heart Bomb (1)

Heart Bomb is a projectile arrow, fired in the direction Cupid is facing. Upon hitting an enemy it damages and slows movement speed by 20% for 3 seconds. A beating heart icon is added to the target and after 3 seconds the heart explodes, dealing damage (and stunning at max stacks) to the target and all enemies within a radius.

Share the Love (2)

Share the Love places three floating hearts at the target location on the ground. Cupid and allies can pick up the hearts, and are healed. Cupid regenerates Mana when an ally picks up a heart. Hearts last for 8 seconds and their healing is increased by 25% of your Physical Power.

Flutter (3)

Flutter dashes Cupid forward increasing his attack speed by a minimum of 10% for 6 seconds, and leaves a trail behind him that increases ally movement speed by 30%.

Fields of Love (4)

Fields of Love is a very large circular area of affect ability cast at a ground location away from Cupid. This ability slows enemies by 35%, cripples (stops escape abilities, such as jumps and dashes) and mezmerize's for 2.5 enemies within the target area. If Cupid has built max stacks for his passive, Fields of Love will apply a stun, not mezmerise. A mezmerise is broken upon taking damage, a stun isn't broken by damage.

Tactics

Building your passive up to full power is important, because it adds a stun to your first and fourth ability. Which gives you extra time to deal damage to enemies.

A powerful combination, after building max stacks is Cupid Fields of Love (4), and then Heart Bomb (1). With max stacks, both abilitys stun for 1 second. And both deal enough damage to take down weaker enemy gods.

Heart Bomb (1) explodes when whatever the heart was attached to dies. This can be useful for stunning enemy gods near their minion wave, by targeting a nearly dead minion. Once the minion dies, Heart Bomb will apply the stun. You will need to have build up the maximum of 8 stacks to have the stun.

Weaknesses

Cupid has the lowest health of any Hunter in the game. However Cupid has Share the Love (2) to heal himself and Flutter (3) for escape.

Playing against Cupid

As mentioned in the Weaknesses section, Cupid has low health and only has one escape ability. When you see Cupid has used Flutter (3), he can't easily escape, so he is at his most vulnerable.

Alternative Items

This section of the guide aims to encourage you away from using common builds from build websites, like the ones above. Item builds aren't static, after the core items (usually the first three items) the other items should be built in response to your enemy. Use the in-game tab menu to see which items your enemy are building.

Ichaival

Ichaival provides +30% Attack Speed and +10 Physical Penetration, as well as giving Cupid a passive buff and debuff. Each successful basic attack increases your Physical Power and reduces your targets item-based Physical Power by 10, for three seconds. The debuff stacks 3 times, for a total of -30 Physical Power.

Odysseus Bow

Odysseus' Bow gives the owner +40% Attack Speed, additonally, every fourth basic attack trigegers chain lightening, which damanges the target and dealing damage to up to 4 other enemies.

Titan's Bane

Titan's Bane adds +33% Physical Penetration and +30 Physical Power. This item is a good counter to an opposition with a lot of physical protection. Use the tab menu in-game to keep an eye on what your enemy is building.

Asi

Asi adds +15% Physical Lifesteal, +20% Attakc Speed and +15% Physical Penetration. If you drop below 35% health, the Physical Lifesteal increases to 25% for 5 seconds, this can only occur once every 15 seconds.

Qin's Sais

Qin's Sais adds +30 Physical Power and +15% Attack Speed. It also deals 4% of the enemy gods maximum health on basic attacks.

Actives

Choosing actives is a mix of complementing your build, your team (builds, abilities and their actives) and in response to enemy abilities. Choosing wisely can alter the outcome of a game.

Greater Purification (Beads)

Greater Purification (sometimes known, simply as "beads") is the third level of Purificaion Beads. Using this item removes Crowd Control (CC) effects and gives an immunity to new CC effects for 3 seconds. They also reduce ability cooldowns by 3 seconds. Lower level beads are as useful, however higher level beads cooldowns are faster and thus more useful.

Salvation

Salvation restores 35% of you and your team mates Mana, and 15% of you and your team mates maximim Health. If you use a lot of mana, or have a spear item slot this solid choice. Typing VVVG in game will shout your team mates to group up, so they can get healed too.

Greater Sprint

Greater Sprint is the third Sprint ability. Using this item increases your movement speed by 40% for 6 seconds and makes the owner immune to slows for the duration.

Aegis Pendant

Aegis Pendant is the third Aegis, it grants damage invulnerability for 2 seconds, preventing you from attacking but allowing you to move.

Weakening Curse

Weakening Curse is the third Curse item, using it reduces all enemy gods movement speed by 40% and their healing is reduced by 65%. Debuffs last for 5 seconds.
