A Guide to Cupid

Cupid is a Pysical Damage Hunter,

Abilities

Lovestruck (Passive)

Each basic attack hit gives Cupid a stack that increases his damaging and healing of his abilitie, stacking a maximum of 8 times. At this maximum Heart Bomb and Fields of Love apply a 1 second stun.

Heart Bomb (1)

Heart Bomb is a projectile arrow, fired in the direction Cupid is facing. Upon hitting an enemy it damages and slows movement speed by 20% for 3 seconds. A beating heart icon is also added to the target and after 3 seconds the heart explodes, dealing damage to the target and all enemies within a radius.

Share the Love (2)

Share the Love places three floating hearts at the target location on the ground. Cupid and allies can pick up the hearts, and are healed. Cupid regenerates Mana when an ally picks up a heart. Hearts last for 8 seconds and their healing is increased by 25% of your Physical Power.

Flutter (3)

Flutter dashes Cupid forward increasing his attack speed for 6 seconds, and leaves a trail behind him that increases ally movement speed by 30%.

Fields of Love (4)

Tactics

Artemis's combo's revolve around first trapping your enenmy, once trapped Suppress the Insolent (3) to call down arrows, and your second ability Vengeful Assault (2) to increase your attack speed. This combo allows you to deal the most amount of damage in the short 2 second stun of the trap.

Weaknesses

It can be easy to miss Calydonian Boar, Artemis's Ultimate. Despite being a relatively large radius around Artemis it can be easy to summon the boar, only to have him sit there because no enemies were in the radius.

Alternative Items

This section of the guide aims to encourage you away from using common builds from build websites, like the ones above. Item builds aren't static, after the core items (usually the first three items) the other items should be built in response to your enemy. Use the in-game tab menu to see which items your enemy are building.

Ichaival

Ichaival provides +30% Attack Speed and +Physical Penetration, as well as giving Cupid a passive buff and debuff. Each successful basic attack increases your Physical Power and reduces your targets item-based Physical Power by 10, for three seconds. The debuff stacks 3 times, for a total of -30 Physical Power.

Odysseus Bow

Odysseus' Bow gives the owner +40% Attack Speed, additonally, every fourth basic attack trigegers chain lightening, which damanges the target and dealing damage to up to 4 other enemies.

Titan's Bane

Titan's Bane adds +33% Physical Penetration and +30 Physical Power. This item is a good counter to an opposition with a lot of physical protection. Use the tab menu in-game to keep an eye on what your enemy is building.

Asi

Asi adds +15% Physical Lifesteal, +20% Attakc Speed and +15% Physical Penetration. If you drop below 35% health, the Physical Lifesteal increases to 25% for 5 seconds, this can only occur once every 15 seconds.

Qin's Sais

Qin's Sais adds +30 Physical Power and +15% Attack Speed. It also deals 4% of the enemy gods maximum health on basic attacks.
