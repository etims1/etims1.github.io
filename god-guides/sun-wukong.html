A Guide to Sun Wukong

Sun Wukong .

Abilities

Undefeated Body (Passive)

When below 35% health Sun Wukong gains 10% Critical Strike Change and +10 Protections, along with additional 1% Critical Strike Chance and Protection per level.

The Magic Cudgel (1)

Sun Wukong slams his Cudgel (weapon) down on the ground damaging all enemies in a line in front of him. Minions and Jungle Camps take +25% extra damage. Damage is increasted by 50% of his Physical Power.

Master's Will (2)

Sun Wukong spins around, damaging and slowing by 30% for 2 seconds all enemies close to him. Damage is increased by 50% of his Physical Power.

72 Transformations (3)

Sun Wukong transforms into an Tiger, Ox, or Eagle and charges forwards. The Tiger mauls the first enemy it hits, stunning for a minimum of 1 second and dealing damage. The Ox knocks back all enemies in its path, causing damage. The Eagle is the fastest and is immune to slows and roots, and is used for escape.

Somersault Cloud (4)

Sun Wukong leaps onto a cloud after a short charge up, leaving a copy of himself to fight on the ground. The copy inherits a percentage of Sun Wukong's streangth and defense. While on the cloud Sun Wukong is invisable and regenerates health. When the copy dies Sun Wukong is revealed on the cloud. The copy is killed instantly if hit by Hard Crowd Control (such as a stun), excluding Banishes and Knockbacks.

Tactics

Weaknesses

Weakening Curse and other anti-heal items can reduce Hercules's healing by up to 65% (Weakening Curse). Watch out for them in enemy builds and be mindful during battles, you might not heal as much as you expected.

Playing against Hercules

When playing against a Hercules players should be mindful of his pull range. Earthbreaker (2) can pull, then Hercules can use Driving Strike (1) to further push enemy gods away from their team, out of their tower radius or into his own tower radius.

Mitigate Wounds (3) at ability level 5 regenerates 90% of the damage he is deal over 10 seconds. If you deal 1000 damage, he will regenerate 900 health over 10 seconds. Unless huge amounts of damage can be dealt to Hercules during a team fight, focus other more squishy players first, until your team can turn all their attention on Hercules.

Alternative Items

This section of the guide aims to encourage you away from using common builds from build websites, like the ones above. Item builds aren't static, after the core items (usually the first three items) the other items should be built in response to your enemy. Use the in-game tab menu to see which items your enemy are building.

The Executioner

Executioner provides +30 Physical Power and +20% Attack Speed. Additionally your Basic Attacks reduce the targets Physical Protections by 6 and an additional 8% for 3 seconds. This stacks 3 times for a total of -18 Physical Protections and remove up to 24% of the targets Physical Protections.

Brawler's Beat Stick

Brawler's Beat Stick provides +40 Physical Power and +20 Physical Penetration, while also making your Basic Attacks reduce enemy healing by 40% and regeneration for 8 seconds.

This is primarily an anti-heal item when having trouble fighting heal hevy gods, such as Hel.

Midguardian Mail

Midgardian Mail provides +350 Health and +60 Physical Protection. Additionally, when hit by basic attacks there is a 30% chance to slow the attackers movement speed by 30% for 2 seconds.

This item is useful against hunters and auto attack heavy gods, they they have a chance to be slowed, allowing to to catch them, or giving you and your allies time to escape.

Mail of Renewal

Mail of Renewal adds +40 Physical Protection, +300 Health and +15% Cooldown Reduction. The passive allows you to gain 20% of your maximum Health and Mana over the next 5 seconds when you get a god kill or an assist.

Actives

Choosing actives is a mix of complementing your build, your team (builds, abilities and their actives) and in response to enemy abilities. Choosing wisely can alter the outcome of a game.

Greater Purification (Beads)

Greater Purification (sometimes known, simply as "beads") is the third level of Purificaion Beads. Using this item removes Crowd Control (CC) effects and gives an immunity to new CC effects for 3 seconds. They also reduce ability cooldowns by 3 seconds. Lower level beads are as useful, however higher level beads cooldowns are faster and thus more useful.

Salvation

Salvation restores 35% of you and your team mates Mana, and 15% of you and your team mates maximim Health. If you use a lot of mana, or have a spear item slot this solid choice. Typing VVVG in game will shout your team mates to group up, so they can get healed too.

Shielded Teleport

Shielded Teleport allows youyou teleport to an allied tower or ward, without being interruptable by damage. It does not provide damage mitigation. Shielded Teleport should be used to travel from a safe place to a tower or ward, enabling you to move across large distances faster.

Weakening Curse

Weakening Curse is the third Curse item, using it reduces all enemy gods movement speed by 40% and their healing is reduced by 65%. Debuffs last for 5 seconds.
