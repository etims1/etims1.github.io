A Guide to Ares

Ares is a Magical Damage Guardian who excells at Crowd Control (CC). His main function is to initiate team fights and disrupt the enemy team, while being able to tank large amounts of damage.

Abilities

Blessed Presence (Passive)

Blessed Presence provides Ares with +30 Magical Power for each completed aura item completed. Aura items are items which provide benefits to the team in an area around a plsayer.

An example of such an item is Heartward Amulet, which provides allies in the area with Physical Protections. Although only two or three Aura items should be built, otherwise Ares's build will likely be inefficient. The point of this passive is to reward players for playing Support properly.

Shackles (1)

Shackles fires a chains in a line in font of Ares, damaging all enemies in the line. Upon hitting an enemy god the chains attach, crippling (preventing them from using movement abilities), slowing Movement Speed by 15%, dealing damage per second and buffing Ares. Shackles last 4 seconds.

After Shakling one enemy Ares may fire another Shackle after up to 2 seconds, he can do this two more times (for a total of three shackles).

Bolster Defenses (2)

Bolster Defenses applies increased Protections (minimum +20) and reduced Crowd Control (minimum 10%) durations to Ares and allies around him. For each enemy god Ares has Shakled (1), the buffs are increased by 7 (21 maximum).

Searing Flesh (3)

Ares fires flames from his shield in a cone in front of him, damaging every .5 seconds for 4 seconds, while also damaging enemies for a percentage of their health (up to 3%). During casting Ares is immune to knockback.

Searing Flesh is best used in combination with Shackles, once Shackled, enemies are easy targets for Searing Flesh.

No Escape (4)

No Escape is cast in a large circle around Ares, chaning all enemies within the area. Chained enemies are pulled towards Ares after 2.5 seconds, taking damage and are stunned for 1 second, increasing by 0.2 seconds per enemy chained. Damage is increased by 40% of your Magical Power.

No Escape is a great initiation ability and can severly disrupt an enemy team if they don't have Crowd Control mitigation, such as Purification Beads.

Tactics

Ares's main combination is to buff himself with Bolster Defense (2), shackle the target (1) and shackle two more times spacing the casting out by 1 second and finally casting Searing Flesh (3). This combination gives Ares extra Protections and reduces any Crowd Control effects on him, applies the maximum amount of slow and cripple time with the chains and deals damage with the final ability. If the enemy has Purification Beads, they can mitigate the Shackle, however if they use those beads, they will be defenseless against Ares's Ultimate, No Escape (4).

Initiating team fights with Ares Ultimate, No Escape, can be devastating. Coupled with the active item Blink or Greater Blink, it becomes even more powerful. The ability to teleport into a group of enemies, use No Escape (4) and have the rest of your team drop everything they've got can be one of the most effective team tactics.

Weaknesses

Ares's damage will mostly be delt over several seconds, if you can keep a target engaged Ares can deal a lot of damage. This is important to remember, as Ares won't be able to jump in and get a lot of kills, not only because he's a Guardian, but his damage is deal over a longer period due to the type of abilities he has.

Playing against Ares

Get Purification Beads to stop you being pulled by No Escape (4).

If Ares baits you into using Purification Beads by using Shackle, there are other item options. Magi's Blessing will absorb a single Crowd Control effect every 45 seconds, an Aegis Pendant (no CC mitigation) gives damage reduction while allowing movement, or depending on the god being played one of their abilities may grant Crowd Control immuity.

Alternative Items

This section of the guide aims to encourage you away from using common builds from build websites, like the ones above. Item builds aren't static, after the core items (usually the first three items) the other items should be built in response to your enemy. Use the in-game tab menu to see which items your enemy are building.

Midguardian Mail

Midgardian Mail provides +350 Health and +60 Physical Protection. Additionally, when hit by basic attacks there is a 30% chance to slow the attackers movement speed by 30% for 2 seconds.

This item is useful against hunters and auto attack heavy gods, they they have a chance to be slowed, allowing to to catch them, or giving you and your allies time to escape.

Ethereal Staff

Ethereal Staff provides +30 Magical Power, +600 Health and +300 Mana. Additionally, it converts 2% of your maximium health into Magical Power. With the 600 Health Ethereal staff gives +42 Magical Power at purchase.

Bulwark of Hope

Bulwark of Hope provides +450 Health and +60 Magical Protction. It also provides a shield activated once you drop below 25% health which reduces damage by 50% for 1 second. The shield can only be activated once every 60 seconds.

This is a useful item to enable Ares to survive attacks against high damage enemies.

Divine Ruin

Divine Ruin gives the owner +50 Magical Power, +15 Magical Lifesteal and +150 Mana. Divine Ruin primarily a anti-heal item, reducing enemies hit by your abilities healing by 40% and health regeneration for 8 seconds.

Very useful in fights against Hel, Hercules and similar heal heavy gods.

Mystical Mail

Mystical Mail adds +300 Health and +60 Physical Protection. While also dealing 40 Magical Damage per second to enemies within 25 units.

Dealing extra damage might sound good, but it comes at a price. While trying to take down a tower with minions, the tower will aquire you if an enemy god gets near you. It can also affect your blink out of combat timer.

Mail of Renewal

Mail of Renewal adds +40 Physical Protection, +300 Health and +15% Cooldown Reduction. The passive allows you to gain 20% of your maximum Health and Mana over the next 5 seconds when you get a god kill or an assist.

Actives

Greater Blink

Greater Blink allows the user to teleport a short distance. On Ymir this item is useful for initiating team fights. Greater Blink is the suggested choice, over combat blink, because while combat blink allows you to blink during combat its cooldown time is 180 seconds. While Greater Blink enjoys a 70 second cooldown, however you can't have been in combat for 5 seconds before blinking.

Shield of the Underworld

Shield of the Underworld reflects 40% of the damage taken (not taking your protections into account) back to the attacker as Magical Damage for the next 5 seconds. During the 5 seconds, enemies can't Lifesteal from you. Not taking your protections into account means you reflect the actual damage you recieved.

Salvation

Salvation restores 35% of you and your team mates Mana, and 15% of you and your team mates maximim Health. If you use a lot of mana, or have a spear item slot this solid choice. Typing VVVG in game will shout your team mates to group up, so they can get healed too.

Weakening Curse

Weakening Curse is the third Curse item, using it reduces all enemy gods movement speed by 40% and their healing is reduced by 65%. Debuffs last for 5 seconds.
