A Guide to Bellona

Bellona is a Warrior, she has high defense, high damage and good mobility. Built with tank and damage she is a formidable foe. Her abilities allow her to iniciate, escape and kill enemies. As a Warrior she is a Physical Damage god, and she benefits from at least one Attack Speed item to increase her healing in battle (see Scourge).

Abilities

Master of War (Passive)

When Bellon is hit by or hits an enemies Basic Attakcs, she gains Protections and Movement Speed for 4 seconds, This stacks a max of 5 times, 3% Movement Speed per stack, 4 protections per stack. For a total of up to +15% Movement Speed and +20 Protections.

Shield Bash (1)

Shield Bash dashes forward and knocks enemies and minions back, dealing damage and slowing by a minimum of 15%, for 2 seconds. Bellona gains 1 stack of Block for every god hit by Shield Bash, this absorbs 100% of damage from a single Basic Attack and reflects 30% back to the enemy. After dashing Bellona weilds a Swords and Shield until out of combat and with every third basic attack she gains another Stack of Block up to 3 stacks.

Bludgeon (2)

Bellona switches to a hammer, spinning, hitting enemies around her and then smashes down damaging in a line in front of her. Each enemy god hit by the spin increases the damage of the hammer smashing down by 30%. After the ability is used Bellona uses the hammer for basic atacks until out of combat. The damage of this ability is increased by up to 60% of her Physical Power.

Scourge (3)

Bellona summons a flail as her weapon and smashes it down in a line in front of her, upon hitting enemy gods they are disarmed for a minimum of 2 seconds, and can't use basic attacks. She continues to use the flail until out of combat, every third hit with Scourge heals Bellona. This means Bellona can use the fail and gain its healing benefits for as long as she can keep hitting enemies.

Eagle's Rally (4)

Bellona leaps into an area, laying a large circle around a flag granting Protections and increased Physical Power to allies in the circle. Enemies near the center of the circle take damage and are stunned for 1 second. The flag last for a minimum of 6 seconds. This ability is useful for catching up with fleeing enemies, initiating attacks and for escaping.

Tactics

A powerful combination is Shield Bash, damaging, slowing and providing Block stacks (allowing Bellona to absorb basic attacks), into Bludgeon, which deals damage, and finally Scourge, which deals damage and heals Bellona.

Eagle's Rally is best used to iniciate fights or to land on top of fleeing enemies. Eagles's Rally along with the combination attack mentioned above will kill or leave most enemies near death.

Using Scourge (3) to heal is an important part of playing Bellona, it allows her to stay near the action.

Using Bellona's Ultimate to escape should be considered if needed.

Weaknesses

Only enemies near the center of Bellona's Ultimate are stunned and damaged when she leaps.

Alternative Items

This section of the guide aims to encourage you away from using common builds from build websites, like the ones above. Item builds aren't static, after the core items (usually the first three items) the other items should be built in response to your enemy. Use the in-game tab menu to see which items your enemy are building.

Ichaival

Ichaival provides +30% Attack Speed and +10 Physical Penetration. Each successful basic attack increases your Physical Power and reduces your targets item-based Physical Power by 10, for three seconds. The debuff stacks 3 times, for a total of -30 Physical Power.

Odysseus Bow

Odysseus' Bow gives the owner +40% Attack Speed, additonally, every fourth basic attack trigegers chain lightening, which damanges the target and dealing damage to up to 4 other enemies. However this item lacks Ichaival's penetration.

The Executioner

Executioner provides +30 Physical Power and +20% Attack Speed. Additionally your Basic Attacks reduce the targets Physical Protections by 6 and an additional 8% for 3 seconds. This stacks 3 times for a total of -18 Physical Protections and remove up to 24% of the targets Physical Protections.

Brawler's Beat Stick

Brawler's Beat Stick provides +40 Physical Power and +20 Physical Penetration, while also making your Basic Attacks reduce enemy healing by 40% and regeneration for 8 seconds.

This is primarily an anti-heal item when having trouble fighting heal hevy gods, such as Hel.

Midguardian Mail

Midgardian Mail provides +350 Health and +60 Physical Protection. Additionally, when hit by basic attacks there is a 30% chance to slow the attackers movement speed by 30% for 2 seconds. This item is useful against hunters and auto attack heavy gods, they they have a chance to be slowed, allowing to to catch them, or giving you and your allies time to escape.

Mail of Renewal

Mail of Renewal adds +40 Physical Protection, +300 Health and +15% Cooldown Reduction. The passive allows you to gain 20% of your maximum Health and Mana over the next 5 seconds when you get a god kill or an assist.

Actives

Choosing actives is a mix of complementing your build, your team (builds, abilities and their actives) and in response to enemy abilities. Choosing wisely can alter the outcome of a game.

Greater Purification (Beads)

Greater Purification (sometimes known, simply as "beads") is the third level of Purificaion Beads. Using this item removes Crowd Control (CC) effects and gives an immunity to new CC effects for 3 seconds. They also reduce ability cooldowns by 3 seconds. Lower level beads are as useful, however higher level beads cooldowns are faster and thus more useful.

Salvation

Salvation restores 35% of you and your team mates Mana, and 15% of you and your team mates maximim Health. If you use a lot of mana, or have a spear item slot this solid choice. Typing VVVG in game will shout your team mates to group up, so they can get healed too.

Shielded Teleport

Shielded Teleport allows youyou teleport to an allied tower or ward, without being interruptable by damage. It does not provide damage mitigation. Shielded Teleport should be used to travel from a safe place to a tower or ward, enabling you to move across large distances faster.

Greater Sprint

Greater Sprint is the third Sprint ability. Using this item increases your movement speed by 40% for 6 seconds and makes the owner immune to slows for the duration.

Weakening Curse

Weakening Curse is the third Curse item, using it reduces all enemy gods movement speed by 40% and their healing is reduced by 65%. Debuffs last for 5 seconds.
