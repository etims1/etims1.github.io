A Guide to Poseidon

Poseidon is a Mage with high damage and mobility

Abilities

Changing Tides (Passive)

Poseidon has a tidal meter above his ability panel. He gains Movement Speed and his abilities do additionnal damage as his Tide Level increases. Successful basic attacks hits increase his Tide Level and using abilities decreases it. At High tide abilities deal up to 20% more damage and Poseidon's Movement Speed is increasd by 10%.

Tidal Surge (1)

Poseidon summons a wave which travels in a line in front of him, damaging and knocking back all enemies hit. Damage is increased +70% of his Magical Power. Tidal Surge decreases the Tidal meter by 35%.

This ability can be easy to dodge because it moves slowly. Using it to clear waves, or in combination with Whirlpool (which applies a slow to enemies) will make most efficient use of it.

Trident (2)

Trident activates a self-buff which grants a Movement Speed increase and making his basics attacks fire 2 additional shots in a spread for 6 seconds. The shots deal 20% more damage, based on Poseidon's Magical Power. Successful shots increases his Tidal Level.

This ability helps Poseidon increase his Tide level quickly. It should be used while your abilities are on cooldown to build the level ready for when your abilities return.

Whirlpool (3)

Poseidon summons a whirlpool at a ground location in front of him, pulling enemies to the center. The whirlpool cripples (prevents movement abilities) and damages every 0.5 seconds for 3 seconds. Whirlpool reduces Poseidon's Tidal Level by 25%. Damage is increased by +15%.

Release the Kraken (4)

Summoned at a target location in front of him, a Kraken appears front a large whirlpool damaging and slowing enenmies caught. Enemies in the center take additional damage, are knocked into the air and stunned. The Kraken reduces Poseidon's Tidal meter by 50%. Damage is increased by 30%, up to 60% at the center of the ability.

Tactics

Keeping Poseidon's Tide level high is important as he deals most of his damage through his abilities. High Tide adds an adiitional 20% damage.

A powerful combinaiton is Whirlpool (3) to cripple, slow and damage, into Kraken (4) to really damage and stun. If the enemy are still alive a Tidal Surge (1) can be used to finish them off. This makes use of that stun, as Tidal Surge moves so slowly.

Below level 5, when Poseidon doesn't have his Ultimate, Whirlpool (3) into Tidal Surge (1) is his main ability combination. However combinations don't have to be used all the time.

If Poseidon is being chased he can drop a Whirlpool (3) at his feet, which will slow the nemy behind him. He can also turn around and use Tidal Surge (1) to knockback and damage the enemy.

Weaknesses

Poseidon's basic attacks have a limited reach. However he is not supposed to be a basic attack god (although he can be built that way). Poseidon's abilities have better range.

Only enemies near the center of Release the Kraken (4) will be stunned. The stun is useful to make it easier to hit Tidal Surge (1).

Playing Against Poseidon

Tidal Surge (1) can be dodge easily due to its slow movement speed.

Purification Beads can remove the cripple, slow and stun of Poseidon's Whilpool and Kraken. Saving Purification Beads for when Poseidon Kraken's you will allow you to escape, however damage will still be taken.

An Aegis Pendant or a different type of Aegis can stop you recieving damage from Poseidon's Ult.

Alternative Items

This section of the guide aims to encourage you away from using common builds from build websites, like the ones above. Item builds aren't static, after the core items (usually the first three items) the other items should be built in response to your enemy. Use the in-game tab menu to see which items your enemy are building.

Spear of the Magus

Spear of the Magus adds +40 Magical Power and +15 Magical Penetration to your damaging abilities. Penetration items remove Protection from your enemy. If your teams composition is mostly Magical, consider building this item, as a smart enemy will build based on your teams composition.

Book of Thoth

Book of Thoth gives its owner +125 Mana, +15 MP5 (Mana Per 5secs) and +100 Magical Power on purchase. This item adds additional Mana for player and minion kills, adding 5 Stacks and 1 Stacks respectiely, up to a maximum of 75 Stacks total. Additionally, 3% of your Mana is converted into Magical Power. At full Stacks Book of Thoth gives the owner +875 Mana, and +126 Magical Power.

Void Stone

Void Stone provides +40 Magical Power and +50 Magical Protection. While also providing an aura which reduces the Magical Protection of enemies within 55 units by 15.

Divine Ruin

Divine Ruin gives the owner +50 Magical Power, +15 Magical Lifesteal and +150 Mana. Divine Ruin primarily a anti-heal item, reducing enemies hit by your abilities healing by 40% and health regeneration for 8 seconds. A useful item when playing against a Hel, Hercules or similar heal heavy god.

Actives

Salvation

Salvation restores 35% of you and your team mates Mana, and 15% of you and your team mates maximim Health. If you use a lot of mana, or have a spear item slot this solid choice. Typing VVVG in game will shout your team mates to group up, so they can get healed too.

Aegis Pendant

Aegis Pendant is the third Aegis, it grants damage invulnerability for 2 seconds, preventing you from attacking but allowing you to move.

Weakening Curse

Weakening Curse is the third Curse item, using it reduces all enemy gods movement speed by 40% and their healing is reduced by 65%. Debuffs last for 5 seconds. This item is useful for mitigating heal heavy enemies such as Hel. If not fighting a healing god, there are better choices.
