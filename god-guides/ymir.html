A Guide to Ymir

Ymir is a Magical Damage Guardian who excells at Crowd Control (CC). His main function is to disrupt, stun and corner, all while being able to tank large amounts of damage.

Abilities

Frostbite (Passive)

All of Ymir's abilities which damange or stun enemies apply the debuff Frostbite. Targets affected by Frostbite recieve 100% more damage from Ymir's basic attacks.

Ice Wall (1)

Ice Wall summons a wall of ice that blocks player movement. As you level this ability its active duration is increased, from 2 seconds to a maximum of 6 seconds. Ice Wall can be used in a multitude of situations: stopping fleeing enemies, protecting allies, protecting your retreat and more.

Glacial Strike (2)

Glacial Strike is a medium length damaging and slowing ability projected in front of Ymir. Damage is increase by 70% of your Magical Power. The slow lasts for 4 seconds.

Glacial Strike can be used in combination with Ice Wall on minions. Laying Ice Wall in front of running minions, allowing them to bunch up and casting Glacial Strike through Ice Wall allows him to hit all the minions. However Ice Wall is an important team defense ability, so it shoul not be wasted.

Frost Breath (3)

Frost Breath is a medium sized cone shaped stun and damaging ability cast in front of Ymir. The stun lasts a minimum of 1.25 seconds, increasing to 2.25 seconds when leveled up. It's damage is increased by 50% of his Magical Power.

This ability is very versatile, it can be used during a fight initiation, or during a retreat. Once frozen Glacial Strike can be used and Ice Wall to stop the enemy retreat.

Shards of Ice (4)

Channeled in a large circle around Ymir for 3 seconds, Shards of Ice then erupts, damaging all enemies within the circle. While channeled it slows enemies by 30% to reduce their ability to escape. The damage delt is increased by 150% of his Magical Power. If enemies get close to leaving the ability radius Shard of Ice can be detinated early by right clicking.

This ability can also be used in combination with Frost Breath, freezing then ulting for massive damage.

Tactics

Ymir with Greater Blink is a frightening foe. The ability to teleport, freeze (3) and ult (4) is a powerful team fight initiation.

Ymir's main combination attack is Frost Breath, Glacial Strike, Ice Wall to prevent retreat.

With level two boots, during online play latency (lag) allows Ymir to look at his feet while running, spawn a wall, and it will appear behind him. This is great for escaping. You will need boots to achieve this. During practise matches, or LAN games latency is too low to allow this, and it will spawn in front of Ymir instead.

Weaknesses

Ymir is slow. Even with shoes he's slow. Just keep that in mind when initiating and protecting.

Playing against Ymir

When playing against a Ymir, press tab to check if they have Blink, you need to be ready for a Blink, Frost Breath (3), Glacial Strike (2) / Shards of Ice (4) combo. This is Ymir's best team fight initiation tactic.

If you are at risk by being frozen by Frost Breath (3) and are already close to Ymir, running behind him can allow you to miss the freeze as Frost Breath is cast in a cone in front of Ymir.

If you can't escape Ymir Ultimate and are going to die, attacking Ymir can help your team mates kill him later, or you could deal enoguh damage to kill him yourself.

Alternative Items

This section of the guide aims to encourage you away from using common builds from build websites, like the ones above. Item builds aren't static, after the core items (usually the first three items) the other items should be built in response to your enemy. Use the in-game tab menu to see which items your enemy are building.

Midguardian Mail

Midgardian Mail provides +350 Health and +60 Physical Protection. Additionally, when hit by basic attacks there is a 30% chance to slow the attackers movement speed by 30% for 2 seconds.

This item is useful against hunters and auto attack heavy gods, they they have a chance to be slowed, allowing to to catch them, or giving you and your allies time to escape.

Ethereal Staff

Ethereal Staff provides +30 Magical Power, +600 Health and +300 Mana. Additionally, it converts 2% of your maximium health into Magical Power. With the 600 Health Ethereal staff gives +42 Magical Power at purchase.

Bulwark of Hope

Bulwark of Hope provides +450 Health and +60 Magical Protction. It also provides a shield activated once you drop below 25% health which reduces damage by 50% for 1 second. The shield can only be activated once every 60 seconds.

This is a useful item to enable Ymir to survive attacks against high damage enemies.

Void Stone

Void Stone provides +40 Magical Power and +50 Magical Protection. While also providing an aura which reduces the Magical Protection of enemies within 55 units by 15.

Divine Ruin

Divine Ruin gives the owner +50 Magical Power, +15 Magical Lifesteal and +150 Mana. Divine Ruin primarily a anti-heal item, reducing enemies hit by your abilities healing by 40% and health regeneration for 8 seconds.

Very useful in fights against Hel, Hercules and similar heal heavy gods.

Mystical Mail

Mystical Mail adds +300 Health and +60 Physical Protection. While also dealing 40 Magical Damage per second to enemies within 25 units.

Dealing extra damage might sound good, but it comes at a price. While trying to take down a tower with minions, the tower will aquire you if an enemy god gets near you. It can also affect your blink out of combat timer.

Mail of Renewal

Mail of Renewal adds +40 Physical Protection, +300 Health and +15% Cooldown Reduction. The passive allows you to gain 20% of your maximum Health and Mana over the next 5 seconds when you get a god kill or an assist.

Actives

Greater Blink

Greater Blink allows the user to teleport a short distance. On Ymir this item is useful for initiating team fights. Greater Blink is the suggested choice, over combat blink, because while combat blink allows you to blink during combat its cooldown time is 180 seconds. While Greater Blink enjoys a 70 second cooldown, however you can't have been in combat for 5 seconds before blinking.

Shield of the Underworld

Shield of the Underworld reflects 40% of the damage taken (not taking your protections into account) back to the attacker as Magical Damage for the next 5 seconds. During the 5 seconds, enemies can't Lifesteal from you. Not taking your protections into account means you reflect the actual damage you recieved.

Salvation

Salvation restores 35% of you and your team mates Mana, and 15% of you and your team mates maximim Health. If you use a lot of mana, or have a spear item slot this solid choice. Typing VVVG in game will shout your team mates to group up, so they can get healed too.

Weakening Curse

Weakening Curse is the third Curse item, using it reduces all enemy gods movement speed by 40% and their healing is reduced by 65%. Debuffs last for 5 seconds.
