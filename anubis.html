A Guide to Anubis

Anubis's primary role is lane clear, in the late game however Anubis has extremely high damage which allows him to take on players more directly. Magical power based items are important to Anubis.

Abilities

Sorrow (Passive)

All of your abilities steal Physical and Magical Proections from your target. Each time you hit an enemy with an ability the amount you steal increases, up to 99% of your item-based Maigcal Lifesteal. 33% per hit, up to three times. While Magical Lifesteal items are important, with only six item slots you should aim to have a balanced build.

Plague of Locusts (1)

Plague of Locusts projects a large, wide cone in front of anubis and damages per tick. The damage is increased by 40% of your magical power. This channeled ability, so it can be stopped by right clicking. While using this ability, you are unable to move. A good tactic against players is to use this in combination with your second ability Mummify (2).

Mummify

Mummify is a projectile stun, stunning for a minimum 1.2 seconds, increasing up to 2 seconds. This ability is Anubis's setup, after stunning an enemy god you use the rest of your abilities.

Grasping Hands

Grasping Hands is a large circular area damage and slow (25%). The damage is increased 35% of your Magical Power. This ability is most useful for minion clearing, or to slow an enemy running away (place in front of them) or to slow an enemy who is under attack to slow any retreat.

Death Gaze

Death Gaze is a laser-like ability, channeled in a line infront of Anubis. It is an extremely damaging ability late game, and can kill most enemy players if the beam is kept on them. This ability is best used once the target has been stunned by Mummify (2).

Tactics

A powerful combinaiton attakc is Mummify (2) to stun, Plague (1) to damage, Grasping Hands (3) which slows and finish them off with basic attacks. At level five Death Gaze (4) will be available and can severely damage or kill most players before Mummify's (2's) stun is over.

Saving Grasping Hands (3) for run-aways can be useful. Grasping hands can be placed in front of a fleeing enemy which will damage and slow, making final basic left click attacks easier to hit.

Weaknesses

Anubis's first (Plague of Locuts) and Ultimate ability (Death Gaze) root him to the ground, meaning he cannot move in any direction, although he can rotate so it is still possible to keep your damaging attacks on enemy players.
